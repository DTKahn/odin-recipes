Lentil Soup

Description

This recipe sure is a real recipe and if you follow it you will end up with some lentil soup most likely! So get ready. It's lentil time. Are you readyt for some soupy lentils? You should be. You were instructed to get ready. So if you're not ready, you're probably not going to end up with lentil soup. This would be the consequence.

Ingredients

  • Red lentils
  • Cumin
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Combine the lentils and rice in a large bowl and then soak in warm water while prepping the other ingredients
  2. Chop the onion
  3. Fry the onion until golden brown to dark brown
  4. Add water and bring to a boil
  5. Drain the onions and rice and then add to the boiling water
  6. Simmer for ~30min until porridge-like consistency
  7. Add salt, cummin, pepper
  8. Portion into bowls
  9. drizzle with olive oil and a slice of lemon
