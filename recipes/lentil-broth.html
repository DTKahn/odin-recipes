Lentil Broth

Description

You know when you really need a broth and you just can't understand why no broth is ever lentilly enough? Well wait no more! This broth will get the job done. That job being adding a lot of lentil flavour to whatever you're making (may I suggest a lentil soup?).

Ingredients

  • Red lentils
  • Cumin
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water â€” like a bunch of it. Get some water.
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Combine the lentils and rice in a large bowl and then soak in warm water while prepping the other ingredients
  2. Chop the onion
  3. Fry the onion until golden brown to dark brown
  4. Add water and bring to a boil
  5. Drain the onions and rice and then add to the boiling water
  6. Simmer for ~30min until broth-like consistency
  7. Add salt, cummin, pepper
  8. Portion into bowls
  9. drizzle with olive oil and a slice of lemon
